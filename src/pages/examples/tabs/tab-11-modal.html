Component Features

  • Title: Modal header title
  • Close on Overlay: Boolean attribute to close when clicking overlay
  • Close on Escape: Boolean attribute to close when pressing Escape key
  • Width: Custom width (e.g., "90vw", "800px", default: 500px)
  • Centered: Vertically and horizontally centered on the page
  • Methods: close()
