An HTML slider with single-value and multi-point support, configurable min/max range, and drag lifecycle events.

Events

  • slider-start: Fires when drag/keyboard interaction starts
  • slider-input: Fires while a thumb is moving (in motion)
  • slider-stop: Fires when interaction ends
  • change: Fires after a stop/commit interaction
